     may revise the allocations of a committee or committees

     aggregates

     and make adjustments to the pay-as-you-go ledger
for one or 
     more bills
joint resolutions
amendments
amendments between 
     the Houses
motions

     for American consumers

     goods
by the amounts provided in such legislation for those 
     purposes
