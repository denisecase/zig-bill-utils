     to consider any bill
joint resolution
motion
amendment

     amendment between the Houses

     forgoes future revenue-raising opportunities through the one-
     time sale of Federal land.
       (b) Waiver and Appeal.--Subsection (a) may be waived or

     fifths of the Members

     vote of three-fifths of the Members of the Senate
duly 
     chosen and sworn

     the ruling of the Chair on a point of order raised under 
     subsection (a).
